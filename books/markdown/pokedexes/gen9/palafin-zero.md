Homebrew Notes
  - This is Palafin's default "Zero Form": a laid-back,
    unassuming dolphin that looks barely different from
    Finizen. Its heroic power only manifests when the
    situation demands it — see the Zero to Hero ability
    and the separate Palafin Hero entry for its battle
    transformation.

Zero to Hero [Basic Ability, Homebrew]
  Frequency: Scene x1 – Free Action
  Trigger: Palafin is recalled to its Poké Ball and
    released again during the same Scene, OR Palafin
    witnesses an ally Pokémon or Trainer at 1/4 of their
    maximum Hit Points or lower.
  Effect: Palafin transforms into its Hero Form. Its
    Base Stats, Capabilities, Skill List, Size, and
    Weight are replaced by those listed in the Palafin
    Hero entry. Moves, Types, Abilities, Nature, and
    Added Stats remain unchanged. The transformation
    ends at the end of the Scene or if Palafin is
    knocked out, at which point it reverts to Zero Form
    and gains the Fatigued condition. Zero to Hero may
    only be used once per Day; the form change requires
    a full night's rest or treatment at a Pokémon Center
    before it can be triggered again.
