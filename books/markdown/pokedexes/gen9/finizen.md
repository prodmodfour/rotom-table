Homebrew Notes
  - Loyalty Evolution: Finizen is a pod-dwelling Pokémon
    that only evolves once it has formed a true emotional
    bond with its Trainer or pod-mates. In PTU terms, it
    evolves at Level 38 while the Trainer has at least 3
    Loyalty with it, reflecting the canon "Let's Go mode"
    bond requirement. GMs may instead ask the player to
    describe a significant moment of shared triumph with
    their pod or Trainer before allowing the evolution.
  - Pack Mon (canonical Capability): Finizen belongs to a
    pod hierarchy and is naturally inclined toward the top
    of that structure. See the core book (Indices and
    Reference) for the full Pack Mon rules — wild Pokémon
    of its unevolved line or at least 10 levels below will
    instinctively obey, and showing a Finizen to wild
    groups imposes a Fearful disposition.
