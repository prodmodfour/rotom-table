Homebrew Notes
  - This is Palafin's "Hero Form" — a towering, muscular
    protector that rises from its humble Zero Form only
    when a friend or innocent is truly in danger. Its
    Attack leaps to an extraordinary 16 Base, rivaling
    pseudo-Legendaries; in exchange, the transformation
    is brief and costly (see Zero to Hero).
  - Hero's Resolve: While in Hero Form, Palafin is
    immune to the Fleeing, Cringe, and Confusion status
    effects, and gains a +5 bonus to rolls made to
    resist Intimidate or Guile checks. If an ally within
    5 meters is reduced to 0 Hit Points, Palafin may
    spend an AP as a Free Action to immediately Shift
    adjacent to that ally and make one Melee Struggle
    Attack or use one At-Will Frequency Move against an
    adjacent foe.
  - Returning to Zero: At the end of the Scene, or if
    Palafin is reduced to 0 HP, it reverts to Zero Form
    and gains the Fatigued condition. A full night's
    rest or Pokémon Center treatment is required before
    it can transform again.
